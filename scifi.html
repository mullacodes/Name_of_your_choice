Fantasy

Fantasy is a genre of fiction involving magical elements, typically set in a fictional universe and sometimes inspired by mythology and folklore. Often, the plot involves magic or witchcraft and takes place on another planet or in another — undiscovered — dimension of this world. The categories of fantasy include High/epic fantasy, Low fantasy, magic realism fantasy, sword and sorcery fantasy, dark fantasy, fables fantasy and fairy tales fantasy.The most popular subgenre is sword and sorcery.

Sci-Fi

SCIENCE FICTION is a fictionalized story wherein the setting and plot are centered around technology, time travel, outer space, or scientific principles, with or without the presence of aliens. Story elements are not found in the known universe and explained by scientific means.The subgenres of science fiction include Hard , Soft , Military, Space Opera, steampunk, Apocalyptic, parallel worlds and dystopian science fiction
